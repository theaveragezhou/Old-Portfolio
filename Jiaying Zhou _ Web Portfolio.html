Welcome

Hello and thank you for taking the time to review my portfolio. My name is Jiaying Zhou and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Professional Writing at Missouri State University. Along with my Professional Writing major, I am also a minor in Classical Humanites with a focus on Greco-Roman cultures. My anticipated graduation date is May 2017.

Experience

I am currently working for Career Specific, the largest food industry job board on the web, as a technical writer. My job responsibilities include researching and updating client information, writing and editing industry articles, writing company descriptions, and creating promotional mailers as needed. Prior to Career Specific, I interned for The Discovery Center of Springfield. At the Discovery Center, my duties include writing and editing the monthly newsletter, updating the Discovery Center website, helping to organize the yearly "Festival of Trees" auction and performing other administrative tasks as needed. I have also worked various positons on the MSU campus. As a summer conference assistant, I was responsible for checking in/out camp participants, updating room condition information, making daily/nightly safety check rounds as needed to various buildings on campus, and other administrative tasks. I also worked as a front desk worker for various dorms. I was responsible for directing resident assistants to where they are needed, handling crisis situation such as fire and tornado drills, and serving as a information source to guests and staff.
